SceneBuilder

SceneBuilder is an open-source GUI designer for JavaFX that is licensed under the BSD license.

Installers are available from Gluon for Windows, MacOS and Linux platforms.

A GUI design in the SceneBuilder app.

All standard JavaFX components are available out-of-the-box with SceneBuilder. In addition, custom components are easily added by importing third-party JAR files.

FXWebApp is fully comaptible with the files created with SceneBuilder which saves GUIs as FXML.

SceneBuilder integrates fully with IDEs such as Netbeans, IntelliJ and Eclipse.
